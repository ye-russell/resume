Overview

This privacy policy describes how this personal resume website collects and uses information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use this website, including:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our site (referrer information)
  • General geographic location (city/country level)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (anonymized)

How We Use This Information

The analytics data helps us understand:

  • Which sections of the resume are most viewed
  • How to improve the website experience
  • General traffic patterns and popular content

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Analytics data is processed by Google Analytics according to their privacy policy.

Cookies

This website uses cookies through Google Analytics to track website usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Rights

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on .

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact me through the contact page.
